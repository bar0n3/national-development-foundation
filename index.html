    NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION

    Charity organization was created as cooperation of Ukrainian and international volunteers, sportsmen, socially active people in order to support Ukraine and overcome the consequences of the humanitarian crisis

    CURRENT DIRECTIONS OF OUR FOUNDATION

    We support hospitals, medical units, and military doctors. This direction is divided into three kinds of volunteering activities: medicine and medical equipment, that most needed in fighting areas, frontline regions, and rear districts)

    We gain humanitarian aid for the centers of refugees. We support the shelters, orphanages, and children from disadvantaged families by clothing, food and hygiene products.

    We organize charity events and educational camps where the children communicate with each other, acquire new knowledge and do physical activities.

    We support animal shelters which were bombed and occupiad by russia in Kiev region. Provide animal food and equipment.

    We help our defenders and military units with necessary things and psychological support. Our team members often visit them in fighting areas and hospitals

    Achievements

    In cooperation with German charity foundations, we have provided medical aid for 30 hospitals in Kyiv, Kryvyi Rih, Odesa, Mykolaiv, and Khmelnytsky regions.

    We sent 50 tons of animal food to the shelters of Kyiv, Volyn, and Khmelnytsky regions. We are actively helping the civilian population affected by russian invasion by evacuating the population from hot spots, delivering food to the elderly, and dispensing the necessary medicines. In cooperation with Volyn charity organizations and Volyn regional council we provided more than 700 requests, 300 tons of humanitarian aid to the different regions of Ukraine
